We're looking for a middle-level designer with experience in similar work. You would work under the supervision of the Lead Designer on Self-Care & corporate web projects for international clients.

Required skills

  • Figma, Framer, UX analysis, UI design, Photoshop,IIlustrator.

Work conditions

  • Full time, full involvement, 8-hour work day.
  • Compensation according to your skill level.
  • A great team.
